Delete Your Account – DBFFitness

If you wish to delete your DBFFitness account and associated data, you can do it directly inside the mobile application. This page explains the steps and the type of data removed according to Google Play requirements.

How to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the DBFFitness app.
  2. Go to Settings → Account.
  3. Select Delete Account.
  4. Confirm the deletion on the popup screen.

Once confirmed, your account will be permanently removed using our secure API.

What Data We Delete

After your request, we permanently delete the following data:

  • Your username
  • Your email address
  • Your fitness profile and any stored personal data
  • Workout preferences and app usage data

Data We May Retain

Some minimal data (such as logs required for security or fraud prevention) may be retained for a short period as allowed by law.
